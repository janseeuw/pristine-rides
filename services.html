Ceramic Coating

Starting at $499

Our ceramic coating service provides long-lasting protection for your vehicle's paint. This professional-grade coating creates a chemical bond with your vehicle's paint, providing superior protection against environmental contaminants, UV rays, and minor scratches.

What's Included:

  • Complete exterior detailing (as described above)
  • Multi-stage paint correction to remove imperfections
  • IPA (Isopropyl Alcohol) wipedown to remove oils and residues
  • Application of professional-grade ceramic coating
  • Curing time in controlled environment
  • Final inspection and touch-ups
  • Detailed care instructions
  • 2-year warranty

Benefits of Ceramic Coating:

  • Superior hydrophobic properties (water beading)
  • Enhanced gloss and shine
  • Protection against UV rays, preventing oxidation
  • Resistance to chemical stains and etching
  • Easier cleaning and maintenance
  • Long-lasting protection (2+ years)

Paint Correction

Starting at $299

Our paint correction service is designed to remove scratches, swirl marks, oxidation, and other imperfections from your vehicle's paint. This process restores the original shine and gloss of your vehicle's finish.

What's Included:

  • Complete exterior wash and decontamination
  • Clay bar treatment to remove embedded contaminants
  • Multi-stage machine polishing to remove imperfections
  • Spot treatment of deeper scratches where possible
  • Final polishing for maximum gloss
  • Application of protective wax or sealant
  • Before and after documentation

Levels of Paint Correction:

  • Level 1 ($299): Single-stage polish to remove light scratches and swirl marks
  • Level 2 ($399): Two-stage polish to remove moderate scratches and defects
  • Level 3 ($599): Multi-stage correction for severe defects and deep scratches

Engine Bay Cleaning
